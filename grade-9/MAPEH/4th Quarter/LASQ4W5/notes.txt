Vocal music is one of the best tools or
expressing one's feelings.

- The parallelisms of music and literature were clearly
evident in almost all forms of vocal music during
Romantic Period.

Composers interpret poems, mood, atmosphere and imagery
into music. Musical compositions such as art song
was written for solo voice and piano.

Opera became an important source of musical expressions.
